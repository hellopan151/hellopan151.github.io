取回截圖的 chrome headless 指令

video list ( B 欄 )

https://player.vimeo.com/video/111

https://player.vimeo.com/video/222

capture file ( C 欄 )

=RIGHT(B2, LEN(B2)-SEARCH("/", B2,30) ) & “.png”

結果會是: 111.png

參數 ( D 欄 )

=“chrome.exe –headless –disable-gpu " & " –screenshot=” & “c:\temp" & C2 & " " & B2

結果會是: chrome.exe –headless –disable-gpu –screenshot=c:\temp\111.png https://player.vimeo.com/video/111
